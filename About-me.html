Who am I?

My name is Laura Sargeant, I am a motivated TAS educator with skills across the disciplines. I love the act of the designing, and that love tends to transcend the medium, this allows me enjoy teaching various subject areas. I personally have a strong interest in Computer based design which includes CAD and coding, as a result I am very competent at using programs such as Fusion360 and Adobe illustrator, and machines such as laser cutters and 3D printers.

In my highschool years I also took part in Hospitality, this has left me with a Cert II in kitchen operations, which I plan to upskills into a cert IV so I can teach this other passion of mine in schools.

What led me to teaching

I can source the moment I discovered my love for teaching back to highschool. I volunteered for the catechism program which meant we would go to local primary schools and teach scripture. During this time I found it very enjoyable and fulfilling planning lessons to engage the students and make the time enjoyable for them.

Once this spark was ignited I began to notice how I would always try and help my classmates and enjoyed doing so thoroughly, this only cemeted in my mind that I could combine my love of teaching with my love of design and be a TAS teacher.
